### Status Colors

- **Online**: #22c55e (Green-500) - Primary action
- **Offline**: #cbd5e1 (Slate-300) - Inactive

### Card Indicators

- **Purple**: Today's earnings
- **Blue**: This month's earnings
- **Orange**: Total trips
- **Green**: Accept/Positive action
- **Red**: Reject/Logout

- **Secondary**: #475569 (Slate-600) - Descriptions
